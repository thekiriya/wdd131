About

This site is a personal digital bookshelf built for readers who like to keep track of what they read. You can add books you’ve finished, rate them, write down what you thought, and include a cover image so your shelf actually looks like yours. Everything stays simple and flexible. Your bookshelf updates as you do, whether you’re logging a favorite, forgetting a book you did not love, or coming back later to remember why one stood out. Over time it becomes less of a list and more of a record of your reading life. If you enjoy seeing patterns in what you read or just want a place where your books do not disappear into the void, you’re in the right place.
